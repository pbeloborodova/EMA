## Data description

After pre-treatment (cleaning and reshaping to long format), the data set contains 23,408 observations collected from 209 participants (112 observations per participant). Each observation represents a record of participant's response to EMA survey. Data were collected in four waves, one week each (see below). Each period of data collection started on Wednesday and ended on the following Tuesday.


```
## # A tibble: 4 x 3
##    Week Start       End        
##   <dbl> <chr>       <chr>      
## 1     1 January 31  February 06
## 2     2 February 28 March 06   
## 3     3 April 18    April 24   
## 4     4 May 16      May 22
```

## Exploratory analysis

### Mising data

There are 42,600 missing values in the data set, 26% of all measurements of dependent variables. The share of participants who did not reply reply to a given EMA survey prompt ranged from 10.53% to 46.89%. Non-response steadily increased throughout the study.

## Multilevel models

Separate multilevel models were created to examine temporal trends in loneliness, connection, and depression. We regressed each outcome on weekly cyclicity (using a sine or cosine term), day of assessment (0 - 27), and diurnal trend (0 - 3).
